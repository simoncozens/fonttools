fontTools.misc.textTools.pad(data, size)[source]¶

Pad byte string ‘data’ with null bytes until its length is a multiple of ‘size’.

>>> len(pad(b'abcd', 4))
4
>>> len(pad(b'abcde', 2))
6
>>> len(pad(b'abcde', 4))
8
>>> pad(b'abcdef', 4) == b'abcdef\x00\x00'
True
